Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

Our projects theme is Fisherman.


There's an animated rolling waves and clouds that drift by. The entire lower background will be filled with blue water that gradually gets darker as the water deepens. There will be a few subtle fish swimming around and bubbles rising in the background. The fishing line will draw your attention down the page.


At the very bottom of the page, beyond the fold and below all the content, will be a surprise...


We own the rights to all the SVG artwork. Our color theme will be derived from the artwork. Transitions will be used on hover effects. Waves, clouds, and fish will be animated. Fishing related icons (fish hooks, etc) will be used as accents to complement the theme.
